Our Partners, Their Success

Every collaboration we form is rooted in trust, care, and a shared mission — to make quality healthcare more accessible. Here’s how our valued partners in the medical community are thriving and touching more lives with us.

Sunrise Multispeciality Hospital

About Sunrise Hospital

Sunrise Hospital, a renowned healthcare center in Mumbai, partnered with us to streamline their pharmaceutical supply chain and ensure the timely availability of critical medicines for patients. They wanted a reliable system to manage stock, prescriptions, and deliveries more efficiently.

How They’re Doing Now

Today, Sunrise Hospital operates with 40% faster medicine procurement, zero shortage incidents, and improved patient satisfaction scores. Their pharmacy operations are now fully digital and seamless.

Dr. Meera Sharma Clinic

About Dr. Meera Sharma

A leading general physician in Delhi, Dr. Meera Sharma joined hands with us to make medicine ordering easier for her patients. She wanted a trusted pharmacy partner who could ensure timely delivery and authentic products for every prescription.

How They’re Doing Now

With our partnership, her patients can now order prescribed medicines online directly through her clinic portal. Repeat prescriptions are auto-managed, reducing wait times and increasing overall patient trust.

GreenLife Diagnostics Center

About GreenLife

GreenLife Diagnostics, a modern testing lab in Bengaluru, was facing challenges managing prescriptions and post-diagnosis medication requests. We provided them with a direct digital link between their reports and our pharmacy services for instant medicine access.

How They’re Doing Now

Today, GreenLife’s patients receive recommended medicines right after diagnosis without extra hassle. Their patient satisfaction rate has risen by 60%, and medicine dispatch time has been cut by half.

CareBridge Children’s Hospital

About CareBridge

CareBridge, a pediatric hospital in Chennai, partnered with us to simplify the availability of pediatric medicines and supplements — a category often hard to stock and deliver quickly.

How They’re Doing Now

With our reliable supply network, CareBridge now fulfills 99% of pediatric prescriptions within the same day. Parents and doctors alike trust the efficiency, ensuring better care for every child.
