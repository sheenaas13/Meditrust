PRIVACY POLICY OF MEDITRUST

MediTrust operates the MediTrust Pharmacy website, which provides the SERVICE of online prescription management, health product sales, and pharmaceutical guidance. This page is used to inform website visitors about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information for anyone who chooses to use our Service.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. The Personal Information we collect is used for providing and improving our pharmaceutical services. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

The terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms and Conditions, accessible on our website unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy.

✧ Information Collection and Use

To ensure safe and efficient pharmaceutical service delivery, we may request you to provide specific personally identifiable information. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and medical prescription details. The information we collect is used to process medication orders, verify prescriptions, communicate health information, and update you on products or pharmacy services.

We may also collect data related to your purchase history and browsing preferences to provide more personalized experiences. For example, this may include recommending over-the-counter wellness products that complement your prescriptions or offering reminders for medication refills. MediTrust is committed to ensuring this data is handled with the utmost confidentiality and only used to improve your health management experience.

✧ Log Data

Whenever you visit our Service, we collect information that your browser sends to us, known as Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type and version, the pages of our website you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other analytical data used to improve our online experience.

This data helps us identify usage patterns and troubleshoot issues that may affect your browsing experience. For instance, if many users experience delays on a specific page, we use this information to optimize the site for smoother performance. None of this technical data is used to personally identify you, and it remains purely for analytical and security enhancement purposes within MediTrust’s systems.

✧ Cookies

Cookies are small data files used as anonymous unique identifiers, sent to your browser from the websites you visit and stored on your device.

MediTrust uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and track website performance. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies. However, please note that refusing cookies may limit access to certain features of our Service, such as saved prescriptions or shopping cart functions.

We also use cookies to understand how users interact with our content and campaigns. This helps us tailor relevant offers and maintain the high quality of our services. MediTrust does not use cookies for any invasive tracking or third-party advertising purposes; every piece of information gathered serves the goal of creating a safer, more efficient, and user-friendly pharmacy experience.

✧ Service Providers

We may engage third-party companies and individuals for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate our Service;
  • To provide the Service on our behalf;
  • To perform Service-related functions; or
  • To assist in analyzing how our Service is utilized.

We want to inform users of our Service that these third parties may have access to your Personal Information. This access is strictly limited to performing assigned tasks on our behalf, and they are obligated not to disclose or use the information for any other purpose.

Our partners include payment gateways, licensed pharmaceutical suppliers, and delivery providers who assist in ensuring that your medications reach you securely and efficiently. Each partner is vetted under strict compliance measures to guarantee that all personal data handling meets healthcare privacy standards and legal requirements within our operational jurisdiction.

✧ Links to Other Sites

Our Service may include links to third-party websites. By clicking on these links, you will be directed to external sites not operated by MediTrust. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites, as we have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of third-party sites or services.

Some of these third-party links may direct you to educational medical content or affiliated health product platforms. While we partner with trusted sources, it’s important to note that MediTrust’s security and confidentiality commitments apply solely to our own platform. Always verify that you are interacting with our official website when sharing sensitive or prescription-related information.

✧ Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with Personal Information, we will take immediate steps to delete such data from our systems.

MediTrust encourages parents and guardians to supervise children’s online activity and educate them about sharing personal details. In cases where medication purchases involve minors (e.g., pediatric prescriptions), all information must be provided and authorized by a parent, guardian, or licensed healthcare provider in accordance with medical regulations.

✧ Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated “last modified” date. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy occasionally to stay informed about how we protect your information.

When substantial updates occur, such as modifications in data handling methods or the introduction of new services, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you through our website or via email. Your continued use of our Service after changes are posted will constitute your acknowledgment and consent to the revised policy.

We value your privacy and welcome your inquiries. If you wish to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you, please contact our privacy team using the above email address.
